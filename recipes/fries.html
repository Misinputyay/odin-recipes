Fries

Photo by DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS

Not feeling like drive-thru? Make your own fries with this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 large russet potato, cut into evenly sized strips
  • Vegetable oil for frying, about 2 cups or as needed
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak potato strips in a large bowl of water for about 30 minutes
  2. Pat with paper towels until thoroughly dry
  3. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or a large saucepan to 135 degrees C. Gently add potatoes to the hot oil and fry for about 5 minutes, stirring and flipping the potatoes occasionally.
  4. Use a slotted spoon to transfer potatoes to a paper towel-lined plate. Let cool completely.
  5. Heat oil again, but this time to 175 degrees C. Add potatoes and fry a second time until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes.
  6. Remove from the deep-fryer and blot with a paper towel. Sprinkle with salt to serve.
  7. Enjoy!
